About the College

Government First Grade College, Shahapur, established in 1983 in Yadgir, Karnataka, is a premier public institution affiliated with Raichur University (formerly Gulbarga University). Accredited with a 'B' grade by NAAC, it offers diverse undergraduate (BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BCA) and postgraduate (M.A., M.Sc.) programs on a spacious 88.17-acre campus.

Key Background

  • Establishment: The college was founded in 1983 to provide accessible higher education in the Shahapur region.
  • Location: Situated in Shahapur, Yadgir district, Karnataka, 585223.
  • Campus & Infrastructure: The campus spans 88.17 acres. It includes well-equipped laboratories, a library with digital resources, and computer centers.
  • Academic Programs: The college offers undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses in Arts, Commerce, and Science streams.
  • Student Strength: The institution serves a large student body, with reports indicating over 2,100 students.
  • Key Focus: The institution focuses on holistic development, incorporating cultural, sports, and social activities alongside academics.
